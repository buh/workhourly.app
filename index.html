Frequently Asked Questions

WorkHourly is built specifically for professionals who work with multiple clients. We include expense tracking per work shift, photo documentation, detailed notes, and professional PDF invoicing. Our smart overtime detection and offline-first design means you never lose data or miss getting paid for extra hours.
All your data is stored locally on your device. You can track time, add expenses, generate reports, and manage everything without any internet connection. Pro users get optional iCloud sync for backup, but the app always works offline first.
Yes! You can add expenses to each work shift, not just each day. This means if you work for two clients in one day, their expenses stay completely separate. Add photos of receipts, descriptions, and amounts - everything is included in your reports.
WorkHourly reminds you at the end of your scheduled work time to set your actual end time. This ensures you capture any overtime. You can also get morning reminders about your work schedule. All notifications work locally without internet.
Pro users get comprehensive analytics including work consistency scoring, client distribution analysis, earning trends with gross vs net comparisons, time efficiency metrics, and seasonal patterns. See which days you work most, which clients pay best, and how your business is growing over time.
Set your regular hours and overtime rate for each client. When you work past your scheduled time, WorkHourly automatically calculates overtime at your premium rate. You can customize rates per client - some might pay 1.5x, others 2x for overtime.
Your data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud container. Only you can access it with your Apple ID. We never see your data - it goes directly from your device to your iCloud account. Even without internet, all your data remains safe on your device.
Start with the free version for one client - it includes all core features like time tracking, expenses, and basic reports. When you're ready for multiple clients and PDF invoices, upgrade to Pro. We offer both subscription and lifetime purchase options.
