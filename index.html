Everything works offline-first, so whether you're in a basement or on a rooftop, your time tracking never stops.

Overtime calculation is where WorkHourly really pays for itself. Set your regular rate and overtime rate for each client - maybe the Johnsons pay 1.5x after 8 hours, but your commercial client pays double time for weekend work. The app asks you to set your actual end time when your scheduled work finishes, so if you worked late, WorkHourly catches every extra minute and calculates it at the right rate automatically. No more lost money because you forgot to track that extra hour, or because you couldn't remember which client pays what for overtime.
